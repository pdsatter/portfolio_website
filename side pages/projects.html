NAE Project

This was a group project for Engineering 1331. This project was about researching on solar energy and creating a matlab program about the economic effects of solar energy production and consumption. There were 5 of us working on it, but I will only focus on my part of the project. I made a function that takes in a dataset of years and the solar energy consumption associated with that year. My function would then start at the first non-zero consumption year and use a exponential equation to predict how solar energy would grow in either 0, 5, or 10 years (depending on what user selects). My equation would also calculate the R^2 value between the original data and my predicted data. I would then graph the original data and my updated data onto a chart (seen below).
